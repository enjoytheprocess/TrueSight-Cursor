## Summary

Design-only persona and requirements for **shared-kitchen / food-bank** inventory — multi-beneficiary stock, donation timing, and recipe guidance for unfamiliar ingredients. **Not** household MVP.

## User story

As a food-bank volunteer, I want shared visibility into what perishables need use soon, so less food is wasted and beneficiaries get workable meal ideas.
